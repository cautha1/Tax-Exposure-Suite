## 4. Run Analysis

When frontend clicks **Run Analysis**:

* fetch company transactions
* fetch active rules from `optional_rules_config`
* run rule-based tax exposure analysis
* optionally clear previously generated open flags
* insert new rows into `tax_risk_flags`
* calculate and return:

  * total_flags
  * estimated_exposure
  * severity_breakdown
  * risk_type_breakdown
  * overall_risk_score
  * overall_risk_level

### Rules to implement

#### VAT risk

* taxable transaction with zero or missing VAT
* suspicious VAT amount vs expected VAT rate
* inconsistent VAT treatment

#### Withholding tax risk

* likely WHT category with missing WHT
* suspicious WHT amount

#### Expense risk

* unusually large expenses
* duplicate expenses
* potentially non-deductible expense categories like entertainment, personal, unclassified

#### Revenue risk

* very large revenue transactions
* missing tax classification

All outputs must be labeled as:
**risk indicators for advisor review**

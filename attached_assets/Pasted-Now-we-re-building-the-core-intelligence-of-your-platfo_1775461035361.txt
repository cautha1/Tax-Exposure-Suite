# 🚀 2. TAX RISK SCORING ALGORITHM (Your Platform)

## 🎯 Goal

Turn raw risks into:

* **Risk Score (0–100)**
* **Risk Level (Low / Medium / High / Critical)**

---

## 🔢 Step 1 — Base Risk Score per Risk

Each risk gets a score:

```text
Risk Score = Severity Weight × Exposure Weight × Confidence Weight
```

---

## 📊 Severity Weight

| Severity | Score |
| -------- | ----- |
| Low      | 1     |
| Medium   | 2     |
| High     | 3     |

---

## 💰 Exposure Weight

```text
Exposure Weight =
1 if < 500,000 UGX
2 if 500k – 5M
3 if 5M – 20M
4 if > 20M
```

---

## 🎯 Confidence Weight (VERY IMPORTANT)

This reflects how certain the system is:

| Confidence | Score |
| ---------- | ----- |
| Low        | 0.5   |
| Medium     | 1     |
| High       | 1.5   |

---

## 🧮 Example

Missing VAT on UGX 10M:

* Severity = High → 3
* Exposure = 3
* Confidence = 1.5

```text
Score = 3 × 3 × 1.5 = 13.5
```

---

## 📊 Step 2 — Company Risk Score

```text
Company Risk Score = SUM(all risk scores) / normalisation factor
```

Normalize to 0–100 scale:

```text
score = min(100, (total_score / total_transactions) × 10)
```

---

## 🚦 Risk Levels

| Score  | Level    |
| ------ | -------- |
| 0–20   | Low      |
| 21–50  | Medium   |
| 51–75  | High     |
| 76–100 | Critical |

---
